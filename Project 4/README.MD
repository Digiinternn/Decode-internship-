# Project 4 - Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## Objective
Analyze sales performance data from January 2023 to June 2025 using Power BI and generate business insights through interactive dashboards.

## Dashboard Summary

### Key Metrics
- Total Revenue: $1.26M
- Total Orders: 1,200
- Total Units Sold: 3,535

### Revenue Trend Analysis
- Revenue peaked in June 2024 at $68,069.
- A strong mid-year seasonal sales pattern was observed.

### Yearly Performance
- 2023 Revenue: $552K
- 2024 Revenue: $488K
- 2025 Revenue (Partial Year): $232K

### Product Performance
- Chair generated approximately $196K revenue.
- Printer generated approximately $196K revenue.
- These were the top-performing products.

### Payment Method Analysis
Revenue was distributed almost equally across:
- Online
- Cash
- Credit Card
- Debit Card
- Gift Card

No single payment channel dominated.

### Customer Acquisition Analysis
Top referral sources:
1. Instagram - 259 Orders
2. Email - 250 Orders
3. Google - 241 Orders

### Operational Insight
- Delivery Rate: 19.2%
- This is a significant operational concern and requires further investigation.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Excel
- SQL

## Key Learning
Power BI enables data analysts to transform raw data into meaningful visual stories that support business decision-making.
